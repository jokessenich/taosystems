Meet the founder

John Kessenich

I started Tao Systems because I kept seeing the same problem: growing businesses drowning in disconnected tools, manual processes, and technology decisions made without strategy.

As Director of Technology for multiple companies, I've built and led teams, rolled out AI automation that eliminated entire roles worth of repetitive work, and managed everything from VOIP systems to security baselines — all remotely.
